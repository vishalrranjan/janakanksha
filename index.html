    WELCOME TO JAN AKANKSHA FOUNDATION

    JAN AKANKSHA FOUNDATION is a reputed non-profit organization dedicated to creating sustainable change in the communities of Jharkhand. Founded with a vision to empower marginalized sections of society, we work across multiple sectors including education, health, agriculture, environment, and rural development.

    At JAN AKANKSHA FOUNDATION, our core philosophy revolves around community participation, sustainable development, and inclusive growth. We implement flagship initiatives like Akanksha Kendra and Gramin-e-Vikas Pariyojana to address critical challenges faced by rural and urban communities in Jharkhand.

    Driven by a team of dedicated professionals and community volunteers, we strive to create lasting impact through our programs that reach all districts and panchayats of Jharkhand. Our approach combines traditional wisdom with modern techniques to ensure holistic development of the communities we serve.

    Your Questions

    JAN AKANKSHA FOUNDATION primarily focuses on community development through initiatives in education and literacy, health and family welfare, agriculture and animal welfare, environment and forests, and rural and urban development across Jharkhand.

    We work across all districts and panchayats of Jharkhand, with a special focus on marginalized and underserved communities. Our flagship initiatives, Akanksha Kendra and Gramin-e-Vikas Pariyojana, are implemented throughout the state.

    You can support us through donations, volunteering your time and skills, partnering with us on specific projects, or spreading awareness about our work. All contributions are utilized transparently for community development initiatives.

    Akanksha Kendra are community development centers that provide integrated services including education, health, and skill development. Gramin-e-Vikas Pariyojana focuses on digital literacy and e-governance for rural empowerment, bridging the digital divide in rural Jharkhand.

    We maintain complete transparency through regular audits, public disclosure of financial statements, detailed project reports, and community participation in planning and monitoring. Our governance structure includes independent oversight and we adhere to all regulatory requirements.
