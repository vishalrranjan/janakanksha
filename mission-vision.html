    Our Mission, Vision & Team

    "Building an empowered, educated, and self-reliant society"

    Our Mission

    Our mission is to build an empowered, educated, and self-reliant society by ensuring that every individual — especially those at the grassroots level — has access to education, healthcare, livelihood, and development opportunities.

    We aim to bring government, corporate, and community efforts together for sustainable social progress. Through our flagship initiatives — Jan Akanksha Kendra and Gramin "E" Vikas Pariyojana — we strive to make every welfare scheme and development service accessible, transparent, and free of cost to the last person in society.

    Core Mission Objectives:

    To promote education, literacy, and skill development for youth and women.

    To improve health, sanitation, and family welfare in rural and urban areas.

    To support sustainable agriculture, animal welfare, and environmental protection.

    To generate employment, entrepreneurship, and digital empowerment through innovation.

    To ensure inclusive development by connecting citizens with government and CSR welfare schemes.

    Our Vision

    Our vision is to create a socially just, economically strong, and environmentally responsible India, where every citizen — regardless of background — can live with dignity, opportunity, and self-reliance.

    We aspire to be a national model organization for social transformation by integrating technology, community participation, and transparent governance.

    We envision an India where:

    No child remains uneducated.

    No family is deprived of health and welfare services.

    Every village becomes self-sufficient and digitally connected.

    Every citizen has access to government benefits and livelihood opportunities.

    Development reaches the last person — truly achieving the spirit of "Sabka Saath, Sabka Vikas".

    Our Team

    The strength of Jan Akanksha Foundation lies in its dedicated and skilled team working across Bihar, Jharkhand, and other Indian states. Our team includes professionals from education, management, social work, IT, and development sectors, who share a common passion for public service and social transformation.

    State Resource Team

    Experienced professionals providing leadership, strategy, and monitoring support.

    District & Block Resource Persons

    Implementing programs and coordinating with local administration.

    Panchayat Resource Persons & Volunteers

    Working at the grassroots level to deliver services directly to citizens.

    Technical & MIS Team

    Managing data, technology, and communication systems for transparency and efficiency.

    Advisory Board & Management Committee

    Guiding the foundation's vision, partnerships, and policy direction.

    Together, this team forms a strong network of changemakers committed to transforming rural and urban India through honesty, dedication, and innovation.

    Our Commitment

    At Jan Akanksha Foundation, we work not for profit but for people. Our commitment is to ensure that every service, every program, and every project we undertake remains transparent, impactful, and free of exploitation — reaching the hearts and homes of those who need it most.
