    Education & Literacy Programs

    ×

    JAN AKANKSHA FOUNDATION is committed to promoting education and literacy across all age groups in Jharkhand. Our education initiatives focus on improving access to quality education, enhancing learning outcomes, and promoting lifelong learning opportunities for community members.

    Key Programs:

    • Non-formal Education Centers: For out-of-school children and youth
    • Remedial Coaching: Academic support programs for improved learning outcomes
    • Adult Literacy Classes: Empowering adults through basic education
    • Educational Infrastructure: Development of school facilities
    • Teacher Training: Capacity building for educators

    Impact:

    • Increased enrollment rates by 35% in intervention areas
    • 70% of students showing grade-appropriate competency levels
    • Established 200 learning centers across all districts

    Health & Family Welfare Programs

    ×

    JAN AKANKSHA FOUNDATION works to improve healthcare access and promote family welfare initiatives across Jharkhand. Our health programs focus on preventive healthcare, maternal and child health, nutrition, and awareness about government health schemes.

    Key Programs:

    • Health Camps: Medical check-ups in remote areas
    • Maternal & Child Health: Specialized interventions for mothers and children
    • Nutrition Programs: For women and children
    • Health Awareness: Campaigns on preventive healthcare
    • Health Worker Training: Building community health capacity

    Impact:

    • Provided healthcare services to over 50,000 beneficiaries
    • Reduced maternal mortality ratio by 25% in intervention districts
    • Trained 500 community health workers

    Agriculture & Animal Welfare Programs

    ×

    JAN AKANKSHA FOUNDATION supports sustainable agriculture and animal welfare practices to enhance rural livelihoods. Our agriculture programs focus on improving agricultural productivity, promoting sustainable farming practices, and enhancing animal health and welfare.

    Key Programs:

    • Sustainable Farming Training: Modern agricultural techniques
    • Organic Farming: Natural farming methods promotion
    • Animal Health Camps: Veterinary services and care
    • High-Value Crops: Diversification and improved practices
    • Water Conservation: Irrigation and watershed management

    Impact:

    • Increased agricultural productivity by 40%
    • Enhanced farmer incomes by 35%
    • Promoted organic farming in 100 villages

    Environment & Forest Conservation Programs

    ×

    JAN AKANKSHA FOUNDATION is dedicated to conserving natural resources and promoting environmental awareness in Jharkhand. Our environment programs focus on afforestation, water conservation, waste management, and promoting sustainable practices among communities.

    Key Programs:

    • Afforestation Drives: Large-scale plantation initiatives
    • Water Conservation: Harvesting structures and watershed management
    • Waste Management: Recycling and proper disposal systems
    • Environmental Awareness: Campaigns and education
    • Biodiversity Conservation: Protection of local flora and fauna

    Impact:

    • Planted over 100,000 trees with 85% survival rate
    • Constructed 250 water harvesting structures
    • Benefited 15,000 households with improved water access

    Rural Development Programs

    ×

    JAN AKANKSHA FOUNDATION works towards enhancing infrastructure and livelihood opportunities in rural areas of Jharkhand. Our rural development programs focus on improving basic infrastructure, creating sustainable livelihood opportunities, and strengthening local governance.

    Key Programs:

    • Infrastructure Development: Roads, drainage, and sanitation
    • Livelihood Training: Skill development and enterprise creation
    • Self-Help Groups: Formation and capacity building
    • Water & Sanitation: Facilities in rural areas
    • Panchayat Strengthening: Local governance support

    Impact:

    • Improved infrastructure in 200 villages
    • Created livelihoods for 15,000 families
    • Formed 500 self-help groups across the state

    Urban Development Programs

    ×

    JAN AKANKSHA FOUNDATION works towards creating sustainable and inclusive urban communities in Jharkhand. Our urban development programs focus on improving urban infrastructure, promoting sustainable urban planning, and enhancing the quality of life for urban residents, especially in slum areas.

    Key Programs:

    • Slum Improvement: Rehabilitation and infrastructure development
    • Urban Services: Water supply and sanitation initiatives
    • Skill Development: Training for urban youth and women
    • Waste Management: Cleanliness and environmental programs
    • Community Mobilization: Participation in urban planning

    Impact:

    • Improved conditions in 50 slum clusters
    • Provided skill training to 10,000 urban youth
    • 70% of trained youth secured employment or started businesses
