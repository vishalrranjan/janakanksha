    Jan Akanksha Foundation Initiatives

    Gramin "E" Vikas Pariyojana

    Under the Gramin "E" Vikas Pariyojana (Rural E-Development Project), all services will be provided completely free of cost to the people sitting at the last level of society — those who are often unaware of or unable to access government and non-government welfare schemes.

    Complete Information Access

    Every eligible individual gets complete information about available welfare programs.

    Technical Assistance

    Beneficiaries receive technical assistance for documentation, online registration, and claim processing.

    100% Free Services

    No charges are taken from anyone — not from individuals, government departments, or private organizations.

    Transparency & People-Centered

    All services remain transparent, and people-centered, ensuring no discrimination or cost.

    The primary mission of this initiative is to make sure that no person in society remains deprived of development opportunities and that every welfare scheme — government or private — reaches its rightful beneficiary without discrimination or cost.
